About Me

Current Position: PhD Candidate at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology

Research Interests: Natural Language Processing, Automatic Speech Recognition, Machine Translation, Artificial Intelligence

I am a passionate researcher and PhD candidate at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, specializing in the fields of Natural Language Processing, Automatic Speech Recognition, Machine Translation, and Artificial Intelligence. With a strong commitment to advancing the understanding and capabilities of AI, I have contributed to several significant publications and projects that push the boundaries of technology.

My work has been recognized with a notable citation count, reflecting the impact and relevance of my research within the academic community[^1^][1]. I have also served as a Research Assistant in the Electronic and Computer Engineering department, where I honed my skills and contributed to the field of speech recognition[^2^][2].

As a dedicated scholar, I am always seeking new challenges and opportunities to further my knowledge and make meaningful contributions to the world of AI. Feel free to reach out to me through my contact information below.
